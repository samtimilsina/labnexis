Our Services.

Detailed insights into our Bioinformatics, QMS, and AI-driven laboratory solutions.

Clinical Lab Quality Management System (cQMS)

Tired of scattered papers and missing IFUs/MSDS? Our digital platform makes your lab paperless, centralizes all records, and lets you access compliance documentation from anywhere.

Cancer Genetics (CGx) Interpretation

Beyond PGx, we use AI models to synthesize research and clinical-trial evidence to interpret the clinical utility of SNPs across oncology and neurology.

Clinical Lab Reporting System (cLRS)

A HIPAA-compliant system that integrates with any EMR. Features built-in Levey-Jennings tools for real-time QC review and Westgard rules analysis.

Remote Compliance Support

Our cloud-based QMS includes proactive support. We track daily activity and review PT calendars to ensure you are always ready for inspections.

Pharmacogenetics (PGx) Reporting

Generate clear PGx reports reviewed by experts. We synthesize literature and trial evidence with predictive AI for maximum accuracy.

Bioinformatics & ML

Custom machine learning models and web applications for drug development and molecular research teams of any size.
