The Team

Who are we?

Lab Nexis is a team of engineers, scientists, and health-data experts dedicated to building digital lab systems that fit real-world workflows. We bring CAP/CLIA-aware rigor to QMS/LRS portals, bioinformatics pipelines, predictive models, and practical AI assistants with human-in-the-loop oversight. We focus on outcomes: cleaner data, faster reviews, and audit-ready evidence.

Ph.D. Computer Science

Dr. Mohan Timilsina

President and Principal Data Scientist

Dr. Timilsina is a trained computer science professional with nearly nine years of computational research and industry experience. He most recently served as a Senior Postdoctoral Researcher at the Data Science Institute, University of Galway. He received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Galway, Ireland in 2020.

His work centers on applied machine learning for tabular and networked data, with expertise spanning supervised and semi-supervised learning, knowledge graphs, and network mining (GNNs). Much of his research is applied to bioinformatics and medical informatics, focusing on building robust, interpretable models that integrate cleanly with real-world compliance requirements.

Ph.D. Medicine (Public Health)

Dr. Meera Tandan

Partner and Strategist

Dr. Tandan is a public health professional and Lead Analyst at the Kidney Epidemiology and Cost Center (KECC), University of Michigan. She previously served as a Newman Research Fellow in the School of Medicine at University College Dublin (UCD). She holds a Ph.D. from the National University of Ireland Galway.

Her work spans chronic disease management, antimicrobial prescribing, and resistance in long-term care facilities. At KECC, she leads analytic projects that inform population health, quality improvement, and evidence-based decision-making for large-scale healthcare environments.
